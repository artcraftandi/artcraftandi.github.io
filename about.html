About Us

Art Craft and I is a social entrepreneurship. A brain child of two passionate individuals combining their expertise in creativity and curriculum design.

Art Craft and I believes in the philosophy that if the process is enjoyable and engaging, the result will lead to growth. We design 'hands on' curriculum for children so that learning continuously happens through the most ordinary tasks and never is cumbersome.

Connections lead to learning and our focus through our endeavour is to help every child we touch, experience the entire journey of creation and cherish the process independently.

On the other hand we believe that a society grows when the weakest links are supported and aided. As a mission we have as far as possible tried to involve the cottage industry in India in procuring the material required for all our activities.

We believe.......

CARE..... CREATE AND CELEBRATE!

 Our Team

Akshada Kamat

Director & Co-founder

12 years experience in the National and International boards has helped me understand learning does not happen in an isolated environment..

Many factors are responsible for the process of learning and contribute to the growth of a child. Also it is a child’s innate ability that decides when and how he processes information and makes it a reality for himself. We as parents, teachers, guardians and friends have the responsibility of exposing them to experiential learning to build positive and proactive experiences. The entire goal of the programme is to consolidate these experiences and build a balanced, skillful, inquiry based and constructive learning process intertwined with attitudes such as empathy, tolerance, compassion to help them become lifelong learners..

“Education is not to reform students or amuse them or to make them expert technicians. It is to unsettle their minds, widen their horizons, inflame their intellects, teach them to think straight, if possible." - Robert M. Hutchins

Priya Patil

Co-founder

Priya Patil is the creative head behind Abracadabra and a Co founder of Art Craft and I.

She is an experienced potter and has over 15 years of experience in conducting student workshops . Prior to this she was the Head of Resources for Pidilite Industries. She started her journey with teaching right after her graduation from JJ School of Arts.She taught History of Ceramics at the exact place where she learnt it. This was followed by stints with Prithvi, Junoon, independent workshops etc.This plethora of experience helped her to understand how children learnt, viewed the world around, processed information, expressed creativity and experienced a sense of joy and accomplishment.

At Hobby Ideas she learned about online markets, DYI kits and content development. Her classes focus not only on art but also helping the participants in developing their own style of expression.

Dorothy Kowli

Communication Head

An educationist for the last 27 years with experience in leading schools like Bombay Scottish, Christ Church and NSS Hillspring International is the Communication Head of the Programme. She states the following about the value addition done by the programme.

“CREATIVITY IN EDUCATION NOW IS AS IMPORTANT AS LITERACY AND WE SHOULD TREAT IT WITH THE SAME STATUS.” Says Sir Ken Robinson. The developmental benefits of Art and Craft are plenty . You will notice the development of motor skills, language, decision making ,visual learning, innovation, cultural awareness and improved academic performance. As a teacher, it is my goal to enhance student learning as a transformative experience. Transformative learning is most likely going to occur when students become personally engaged with the material and perceive the subject matter to be directly relevant to their own lives. Teaching and learning encompasses more than just thinking. Hence it is important to help the child move from the simplest to the most complex which means to help the child to understand, apply , analyse, evaluate and create. Art Craft and I aims at developing all the above mentioned skills with this skill based programme.
